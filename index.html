Tech Quiz

Gameplay:

  • Tech quiz consist of three rounds.
  • Only round one will be purely technical round. Round two and three will consist of GK as well as technical questions.

Round 1:

  • Written test for each team, 10 multi choice questions, 2 marks each.
  • Total time 10 minutes, no negative marking.
  • Eight teams will be selected.

Round 2:

  • Round two will be buffer round.
  • Each team will be provided a buzzer.
  • This round will consist of 10 questions.
  • With every correct answer, the team is provided with 2 points and every wrong answer gets a penalty of 1 point.
  • Two teams with maximum points will qualify for the third round.

Round 3:

  • 3rd round is the final round.
  • Only two teams will be competing for the 1st place
  • Each team must answer 10 questions.
  • With every correct answer, the team is provided with 2 points and every wrong answer gets a penalty of 1 point.
  • In case of a pass the opponent team gets to answer the question, correct answer fetches the opponent team 2 points.
  • The team with the highest points will be the winner.

Rules:

  • Quiz to take place in groups.
  • Each group can have maximum 3 members.
  • The decision of the quiz master will be final.

Entry fee: 60₹

Contact:

detroix@kccemsr.edu.in
